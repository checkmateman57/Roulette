# Roulette
This is a text-based game of Roulette, written entirely in python. There's only one 0, and not 00, and according the internet, this is European roulette because of that. Don't worry guys, I'm giving you better odds. 👍

Happy Gambling!!!
# Features
All of the standard bets in Roulette apply, but you can only do 1 bet in that section:

Single Number bets - bet on 1 number

2 Way Splits - bet on 2 numbers that border each other horizontally or vertically

3 Way Row - Bet on any Row

4 Way corner - bet on any 4 numbers that share a corner

6 Way Double Street - Bet on any 2 consecutive Rows

Colours - The average Red or Black that is most commonly bet upon

High/Low - Over or Under 18.5? Not including 0

Odd/Even - Is the number odd or even? 0 not included

Thirds - Bet on the low, middle, or high 12 numbers. 0 not included

Columns, 3 columns of 12 numbers, 0 not included
